OVER VIEW

The sporty-looking BMW X6 may not be as roomy as more conventional SUVs. But with its powerful engines and luxurious interior, the X6 delivers an experience that's both stylish and fun. The BMW X6 is a 5-seater vehicle that comes in 2 trim levels. The most popular style is the xDrive40i. This X6 is estimated to deliver 21 MPG in the city and 25 MPG on the highway.

price starting at : $67,350

FEATURES

The biggest news for the 2022 model year is that the base rear-wheel-drive X6 sDrive40i has been dropped from the lineup. The xDrive40i all-wheel-drive version of this model becomes the new entry-level car. Wireless charging is no longer a standard fitment and other extras and option packages have been shuffled around a bit. For example, the Climate Comfort package has been reconfigured and the Convenience package is no longer available for the base car. The Premium and Executive packages have also seen changes.
